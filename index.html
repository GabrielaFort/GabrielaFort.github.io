About Me

I am a computational cancer biologist and postdoctoral fellow integrating large-scale genomics, real-world data, and large language models to advance clinical oncology research. I bring over six years of interdisciplinary experience spanning molecular biology, genomics, bioinformatics, and data science. I am also passionate about science communication and skilled in conveying complex scientific concepts to diverse audiences.

I am particularly interested in applying AI to clinical research to enhance data analysis, interpretation, and communication. My goal is to leverage computational approaches to uncover novel insights into cancer biology and ultimately improve patient outcomes.

Current Focus

  • Designing AI tools for analysis of clinical and genomic data
  • Integrating real-world and molecular datasets in oncology research
  • Building computational frameworks to generate actionable insights and support translational research
